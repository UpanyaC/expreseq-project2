How It Works

Simple for users. Hard for intruders.

ExpreseQ creates a live human verification layer between your apps and unauthorized access.

01

Add the app

Select the app you want to protect inside ExpreseQ and create a dedicated security profile for it.

02

Enroll your expression

Record your live facial expression so ExpreseQ can capture identity, expression pattern, and liveness cues.

03

Challenge at access time

When someone tries to open the protected app, ExpreseQ requests a fresh live verification.

04

Verify or block

If the live input matches, access is granted. If not, the attempt is blocked and logged as suspicious.
